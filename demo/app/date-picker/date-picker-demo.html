 Date Picker & DateTime Picker

Date Picker :

Selected date : {{theDatePicker | date:'yyyy-MM-dd'}}

DateTime Picker :

Selected date : {{theDatetimePicker | date:'yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss'}}

Shortcut navigation :

  • Inside input :
    • Press D to select the current date.
    • Press ↑ to increase the selected date of 1 day/hour.
    • Press ↓ to decrease the selected date of 1 day/hour.
    • Press Tab to switch focus from day to hour.
    • Press Shift + Tab to switch focus from hour to day.
  • Inside date selector :
    • Press ↑ to decrease the selected date of 7 day.
    • Press ↓ to increase the selected date of 7 day.
    • Press ← to decrease the selected date of 1 day.
    • Press → to increase the selected date of 1 day.

 Keyboard Operations

DateTime Picker with 30 minutes as unit :

Selected date : {{kboDate | date:'yyyy-MM-dd HH:mm:ss'}}

Shortcut navigation :

  • Inside input :
    • Press D to select the current date.
    • Press ↑ to increase the selected date of n unit.
    • Press ↓ to decrease the selected date of n unit.
  • Parameters :
    • [stepUnit]
      The type of unit as in StepUnit enum.
      (default to days)
    • [stepValue]
      The amount of unit applied by the operation.
      (default: 1)
    • [stepRoundToMinutes]
      Whenever the minutes have to be rounded.
